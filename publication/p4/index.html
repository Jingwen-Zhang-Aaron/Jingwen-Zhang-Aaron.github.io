(CN) A Low-Temperature Measurement Apparatus of Resonant Ultrasound Spectroscopy (RUS) (一种超声共振谱仪低温测试装置)

Jingwen Zhang, Shao Yang, Kefu Yao
December 2017
PDF Source Document

Abstract

Resonant ultrasound spectroscopy (RUS) is a novel and reliable dynamic method, using the information of resonance of the specimen to calculate the elastic moduli. The research for elastic moduli is the key point to get insight knowledge of glassy materials. Although there has been low temperature apparatus of RUS for low temperature RUS experiments, they have not yet been made public.
